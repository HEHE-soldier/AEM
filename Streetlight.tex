\section{Streetlight}
\subsection{Introduction}

The demand for energy is expected to increase by 25\% in 2040. With the surge of interest in renewable energies, Asia in particular has seen greater demand by up to 60\% with the majority coming from developing countries. Solar power has surpassed wind in installed capacity and is expected to replace hydroponics by 2030 and coal by 2040. Hence the demand grows as people find more functionable and suitable sources of energy (Institute for Energy Research, 2018). Streetlights play a major role in lighting city streets and dark rural areas. Energy-efficient lightbulbs already show promise, cost efficient practices by using LED lights for streetlights have shown results in reducing electricity consumption by up to 300,000 kwh per month (City Government of Baguio, 2022; Department of Energy, 2025; CHZ Lighting Technology, 2025; Fonda Tech, 2025).
